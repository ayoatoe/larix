Infectious Diseases and Vaccines

There is a constant need for ongoing research, innovation and investment in new technologies and treatment options to ensure that we are equipped to meet the evolving threats posed by infectious diseases. With the fast pace of infectious disease and vaccine trials, our experienced team knows how to ensure the logistics are in place to allow for accelerated enrollment. We’ve supported a wide variety of global vaccine trials across adult and patient populations, including COVID-19. Another major achievement in infectious disease research is the development of new antibiotics and antiviral drugs. The introduction of new drugs, combined with improved vaccination programs has led to a significant reduction in mortality from infectious diseases such as HIV and tuberculosis.
